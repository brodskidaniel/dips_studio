Who I Am

DIPS is founded and run by a highly driven creator with a deep passion for building interactive systems, games and experimental technology. I care about structure, creativity, curiosity and teamwork — and I build things because I genuinely love the process.

DIPS is not about chasing money. It’s about learning together, creating meaningful projects, becoming better developers and forming real friendships along the way.
