How We Work Together

DIPS grows slowly and intentionally. We focus on trust, communication and long-term teamwork.

01

Getting Along & Learning Together

We start by getting to know each other. Learning how to communicate, cooperate and work smoothly without friction.

02

Finding the Team Flow

We build rhythm and respect. Everyone works at their own pace, solo or in small groups.

03

Slow & Passive Growth

When projects start earning, we move carefully. No rush. No pressure. Only sustainable progress.

04

First Released Games

We release games and evaluate naturally. Promotion is optional — decisions are data-driven and calm.

05

The Main Project Begins

Once aligned and experienced, we begin our large flagship project — built on everything we’ve learned together.

We do not work for money in the beginning.

We work because we enjoy creating together.

The team is small (around 5 people), flexible and trust-based.
